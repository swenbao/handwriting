current to be 0.01 mA and the maximum current to be 20 mA.

LED is lighted and 0.05 step after the LED is lighted (the light is very weak). Stop the voltage after
the current exceed 20 mA. Note: the current needs to be lower than 20 mA and the voltage needs to be lower than 3 V.

and this function to be the “bulit-in voltage” of the LED.
